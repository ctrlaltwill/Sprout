---
title: "Burying Flashcards"
---


Last modified: 30/03/2026

## Purpose

Burying removes a flashcard from the current session and brings it back after 24 hours.
It does **not** reset or change scheduling values (interval, stability, difficulty).

## Bury Steps

During review:

- press `B`, or
- open the More menu and choose **Bury**.

If you skip the same flashcard three times in one session, LearnKit offers a **Bury for today?** prompt.

## Return Rule

Buried flashcards return to the review queue 24 hours after they were buried.

## Find Buried

Open [Flashcard Library](../Flashcard-Library) and filter Stage = Buried.

## Limits

- Burying is temporary (today only).
- Burying does not delete flashcards.
- For long-term removal from review, use [Suspending Flashcards](../Suspending-Flashcards).
