Coach helps you turn a large study scope into a realistic day-by-day plan.

Use it when you are studying toward an exam, deadline, or fixed target date and want LearnKit to help pace your flashcards, note review, and test practice.

## When To Use Coach

Coach is most useful when:

- you have a lot of material and a fixed deadline
- you want daily study targets
- you want one place to launch flashcards, notes, and tests from the same plan

## Create A Plan

1. Open **Coach**.
2. Click **Get started** or create a new plan.
3. Choose the notes, folders, groups, or broader scope you want to include.
4. Set an exam or target date.
5. Choose an intensity level.
6. Review the summary and start the plan.

Coach then calculates daily targets based on how much material is in scope and how much time remains.

## What You Can Do From A Plan

From a Coach plan you can:

- study flashcards for that plan
- review notes for that plan
- generate a practice test for that plan

This makes Coach a workflow hub, not just a dashboard.

## Daily Progress

Each plan shows how much of today’s target you have completed and what remains.

That helps answer one practical question quickly: what should I do next today?

## Health And Readiness

Coach estimates readiness by combining:

- how much material you have actually studied
- how well retained that material currently is
- how feasible the remaining workload is before the deadline

You will usually see separate health measures for flashcards, notes, and overall exam readiness.

## Best Practices

- start early if possible
- study consistently instead of cramming
- review weak areas before generating more material
- lower intensity if the daily target is unrealistic
