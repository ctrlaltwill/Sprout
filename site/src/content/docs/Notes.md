# Notes

LearnKit works best when your notes stay at the center of the workflow.

You write in Markdown first, then turn the most useful parts into study material.

## Notes-First Workflow

The basic flow is:

1. write or refine a note
2. add flashcard content where needed
3. [Sync](./Syncing) your database
4. review the resulting flashcards
5. come back and improve the source note over time

## Why Notes Matter

Notes are not just a place where flashcards start.

They are the source of truth for your material. That means a good note structure usually leads to cleaner flashcards, easier edits, and fewer sync problems.

## Good Habits

- keep headings clear and predictable
- make prompts concise and answers specific
- avoid overly dense flashcards pulled from messy paragraphs
- fix source notes instead of patching around bad flashcard output later

## If Cards Do Not Parse Properly

Check:

- [Flashcard Formatting](./Flashcard-Formatting)
- [Custom Delimiters](./Custom-Delimiters)
- [Settings](./Settings)

Then [Sync](./Syncing) your database again.
