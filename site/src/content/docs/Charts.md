This page explains the charts shown inside [Analytics](../Analytics).

## Flashcard charts

### Review Calendar Heatmap

Shows daily flashcard review activity across the past year.

Use it to spot streaks, missed days, and unusually heavy review sessions.

### Card Stage Distribution

Shows how your flashcards are split across stages such as new, learning, review, relearning, and suspended.

This is useful for seeing whether your collection is still intake-heavy or has matured into long-term review.

### Study Forecast

Shows how many flashcards are due over the next 30 days.

Use it to spot upcoming workload spikes before they land.

### Answer Buttons

Shows the distribution of review grades over the last 30 days.

If `Again` is high, that usually means the cards are too hard, too vague, or too dense.

### New Cards Per Day

Shows how many new flashcards you added each day.

This helps you compare card creation pace with review capacity.

### Stability Distribution

Shows the spread of current stability values across your flashcards.

Lower stability usually means shorter intervals and more fragile recall.

### Forgetting Curve

Compares observed retention with the expected FSRS curve.

If your observed retention is consistently below expectation, review your material quality and [Scheduling](../Scheduling).

## Notes chart

### Note Review Activity

Shows scheduled note reviews against completed note reviews over time.

This is useful for checking whether your note-review system is being kept up with, or whether scheduled reviews are being skipped.

## Tests chart

### Tests Performance

Shows test-attempt scores over time, including daily averages.

This is useful for seeing whether exam performance is improving and whether recent attempts are trending up or down.

## Reading charts well

- look for trend direction, not one isolated day
- compare forecasted load with your actual study capacity
- use stage and stability charts together when diagnosing workload
- use tests and notes charts to catch neglected workflows outside flashcard review
