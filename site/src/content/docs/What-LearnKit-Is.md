title: "What LearnKit Is"
---


LearnKit is an Obsidian-native study system that turns your notes into material you can actively review.

It combines flashcards, note review, test generation, exam coaching and FSRS scheduling so your vault becomes a place to learn from, not just store information.

## What You Can Do With LearnKit

- Create flashcards directly inside Markdown notes.
- Review cards with FSRS-based scheduling.
- Study notes and cards as part of the same workflow.
- Generate practice tests from your material.
- Track progress with coaching and analytics tools.
- Use Companion features for AI-assisted study workflows.

## Who It Is For

LearnKit is best for people who already keep study material in Obsidian and want revision to happen in the same vault.

It works especially well if you want to:

- write notes first, then turn parts of them into cards
- keep spaced repetition close to your source material
- combine flashcards, note review, and tests in one system

## How LearnKit Works

The core model is simple:

1. Write or edit notes.
2. Add card content where needed.
3. [Sync](../Syncing) your database.
4. Review cards in [Study Sessions](../Study-Sessions).
5. Improve your material over time using feedback from review.
